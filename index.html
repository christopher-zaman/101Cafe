101 Cafe







101 Cafe is a family owned and operated restaurant located in Ozone Park, Queens NY. We feature 100% ground colombian coffee, flame broiled burgers and a variety of fresh salads. We also have Greek specialties such as gyros and spinach pies as well as many other diner classics. We strive on providing fresh and delicious food in a family friendly atmosphere. Come on in and see for yourself!
